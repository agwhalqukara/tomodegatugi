Who is Nikki Bon?

Nikki Bon is an American/Canadian comedian, actress, and writer known for her unique blend of unhinged, self-deprecating, and dark humor.

Where did she grow up?

She was raised in Toronto with her single mother and grandmother until a stepfather joined their family when she was 11.

How did she start her career?

She began acting at 8, using her roles to scare away her mother’s boyfriends. This passion for performance led her to study acting and improv, training at The Upright Citizens Brigade (UCB) and the Atlantic Acting School.

What notable schools did she attend?

She pursued acting at Queens University, obtaining a BAH in Theatre, trained at Toronto’s Second City, UCB in New York, and the Atlantic Acting School, and completed the Groundlings writing lab.

When did she move to Los Angeles?

She relocated to Los Angeles in 2014, continuing to hone her comedy and acting skills.

What are some of her notable performances?

She has performed stand-up across North America, featured on season 14 of America’s Got Talent as Insta Girl, and taken part in various improv shows.
