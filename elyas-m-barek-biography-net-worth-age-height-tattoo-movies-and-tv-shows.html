Elyas M’Barek is a German-speaking actor of Tunisian and Austrian descent.

Elyas M’Barek was born on 29th May, 1982 to an Austrian mother and Tunisian mother in Munich. Not much is known about Elyas M’Barek’s childhood except that he finished school at the age of 22.

Elyas M’Barek Height

Elyas M’Barek Movies and TV Shows

Elyas M’Barek began acting in 2001 in Dennis Gansel’s sex comedy, Madchen, Madchen. Elyas M’Barek is famed for playing the role of a Turk, Cem Öztürk in Turkisch fur Anfanger. Elyas M’Barek also acted in the TV series “Abschnitt 40”.

In 2008, Elyas M’Barek starred in Gansel’s acclaimed drama film Die Welle, playing the role of Sinan, a student who embraced fascism.

Then in 2012, Elyas M’Barek reunited with Madchen Madchen and Die Welle co-star Max Riemelt in the drama film Heiter bis Wolkig.  Elyas M’Barek also played a lead in the feature film, “Türkisch für Anfänger”.

In 2013, Elyas M’Barek played the role of a vampire in The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones. Again, in 2013, Elyas M’Barek starred in Fack ju Gohte, which was seen by three million people in 17 days and as a result, became the most successful German film of the year. In the same year, Elyas M’Barek played a supporting role in the adventure movie, The Physician which also became a box-office hit in Germany.
