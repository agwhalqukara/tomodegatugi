John Wick: Chapter 4 Is It Renewed?

John Wick: Chapter 4 has been Confirmed it is renewed for March 2023.

John Wick: Chapter 4 Release Date Confirmed

Fans of Keanu Reeves’ titular hitman will have to wait a bit longer for the next part as his quest for vengeance continues.

The action film’s release date has been pushed back about a year from its original May 27, 2022 release date, according to Lionsgate. According to a Twitter video, “John Wick: Chapter 4” will now be released in theatres on March 24, 2023.

John Wick: Chapter 4 Cast

The cast includes Reeves, Donnie Yen, Laurence Fishburne, Lance Reddick, Bill Skarsgard, Rina Sawayama, Shamier Anderson, and Ian McShane and is directed by Chad Stahelski. The screenplay was written by Michael Finch and Shay Hatten, with Stahelski, Basil Iwanyk, and Erica Lee directing and Reeves and Louise Rosner serving as executive producers. The “John Wick” franchise has grossed over $584.9 million worldwide, with “Chapter 3 – Parabellum” garnering the franchise’s highest-grossing $327 million.

John Wick: Chapter 4 Synopsis

An examination of the exploits, hazardous encounters, and adventures of the famed hitman John Wick.

John Wick: Chapter 4 Plot

The assassin was pulled out of retirement to avenge his unfortunate deceased pet when John Wick first came out in 2014. He had to fulfill an old blood oath in John Wick: Chapter 2. After then, in John Wick: Chapter 3 – Parabellum, he engaged in combat with the High Table; hence, the next step for the ruthless assassin is unknown.

The Tick-Tock Man (Jason Mantzoukas) and Bowery King (Laurence Fishburne) took a seriously wounded John Wick to an underground bunker where they agreed on their common wrath at the High Table in Chapter 3.

Winston (Ian McShane) wanted to keep his job as manager of The Continental and with the High Table, so he left John for dead. So, in Chapter 4, we’re hoping to see Bowery King and John Wick join forces.

We also understand that the crew has been filming in France, Germany, New York, and Japan, so expect plenty more intercontinental brawls.

According to Chad Stahleski of Collider, the film will act as a wrap-up for the series.

“I think Chapter 4 is a good continuation of the three previous films, and it has a little bit of a finale to it,” Stahleski said. “We introduce a slew of new people who have a lot to say about John Wick, both in the past and, obviously, now.” I believe it is optimism and brotherhood. It’s what life would be like with John in the midst of an existential crisis, and I believe that’s the most enjoyable aspect of the film.”
